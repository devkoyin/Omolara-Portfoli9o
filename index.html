Blender · Concept Idea
BEHIND THE BUILD
A storytelling idea I'd love to bring to Blender

If given the opportunity to join Blender, one of the first things I would want to explore is a content series called Behind the Build — a way of making the open-source creative process visible, human, and compelling to audiences beyond the existing community.

The idea is simple: every Blender project has two stories — the final result, and the journey behind it. The experiments, the decisions, the people, the iterations. That second story rarely gets told. I would want to change that through short blog features, social content, and community spotlights that bring Blender's process to life.

Sample Post Concept

Every Blender project begins long before the final render. From early experiments to community feedback — the process is where open-source creativity truly comes alive. Behind the Build would bring those stories forward.

#Blender · #OpenSource · #BehindTheBuild · #CreativeProcess
